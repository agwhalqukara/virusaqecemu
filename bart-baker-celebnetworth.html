Bart Baker Biography

Bart Baker is best known as Parody Artist who has an estimated Net Worth of $3 Million. YouTuber who rose to fame for releasing parody songs such as “I Knew You Were Trouble.” His self-titled channel has earned more than 10 million subscribers. He is also known for his short-form content on TikTok, where he has gained more than 5 million fans.  He was born on May 5,1986, Chicago, IL. His most popular YouTube videos include “California Boys” and “Hot Dog, Condom Style.” He posted a video with JoJo Siwa and Hayden Summerall from Playlist Live Orlando in 2017.  He grew up in Winnetka, Illinois and graduated with a degree in film from the University of Miami.   He created a parody of Miley Cyrus' song “Wrecking Ball.”  His parents are Walter and Janet Baker. He began a relationship with Shira Lazar in 2014. He and Shira frequently collaborate together across social media and in 2017, they bought a baby golden retriever named Bentley.  His zodiac sign is Taurus
