Bella Thorne Comes Out As Bisexual

Bella Thorne came out to her followers on Twitter earlier this afternoon.

A fan simply asked Bella if she was bisexual, and the actress gave a short, sweet, simple answer – “Yes.”

Immediately after breaking the news, Bella was flooded with support from fans, supporters, and celebrities alike – along with a number of marriage proposals.

The news might come as a bit of a surprise to fans, especially since she only just ended her year long relationship with Gregg Sulkin last week. Their relationship was quite public, filled with constant outings to cute Instagram shots of the two together, so the break-up was quite unexpected, but she assured fans that both are still friends.

Either way, all of us at @CelebMix are super happy for Bella and we’re so glad she has the chance to show the world her true self!
