CHRIS BROWNS DAUGHTER, ROYALTY, POSES IN HOLIDAY PHOTO WITH HER MOM

Chris Brown’s daughter, Royalty Brown, is stunning in new holiday photos with her mother, Nia Guzman, and younger sister, Sinatra.

“Happy holidays from us to you!” a message reads on Royalty’s Instagram page.

The relationship between Chris Brown and Nia Guzman was one of his least publicized relationships.

The pair share one child in common, Royalty, who was born in May of 2014. Yet, the news of Brown’s firstborn child didn’t become public news until 2015 due to the fact that Brown didn’t realize that he was the father.

Brown and Guzman have had a difficult road as co-parents. If you remember, in 2018, the pair underwent an intense legal battle over child support. However, since then, they have found common ground. 

“Chris gets along great with Nia because they decided a long time ago to make things work for the sake of Royalty,” a source shared in an exclusive interview with HollywoodLife in March of this year.

As for Brown, Royalty is “the most important girl in his life and he is willing to do whatever it takes to show her how much her parents love her and that she sees how well they get along.”

In addition to being the apple of her father’s eye, the adorable 6-year-old is also capturing the hearts of the fashion industry as she and her little sister have become the latest models for Fashion Nova’s new children’s line, Nova Kids, which was launched in late September of this year.

“Nova Kids by Fashion Nova is now live,” Royalty exclaimed on her own Instagram page, announcing the news. “Go follow and shop Nova Kids for the trendiest, comfiest, and cutest kids’ clothes.”

Brown’s adorable daughter’s Fashion Nova advertisement for the new Nova Kids line is just one of her of many business ventures in which she is involved.

To date, Royalty has offered consumers apparel and accessories, including “mommy and me” swimwear and it doesn’t look like she is slowing down anytime soon.

Congratulations to Nia and Chris. It looks like getting on the right parenting path has opened more doors than one for Royalty to truly thrive.
