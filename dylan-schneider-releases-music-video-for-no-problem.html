Dylan Schneider releases music video for 'No Problem'

Country artist Dylan Schneider has released the music video for his current single, ‘No Problem’.

Directed by Emmy Award winner Roman White (Taylor Swift, Justin Bieber, Carrie Underwood), the visual sees Dylan in an abandoned farmhouse surrounded by pictures of him and his on-screen love interest. Throughout the visual, the beautiful girl ‘ghosts’ Dylan by appearing then disappearing, leaving him to struggle with his feelings.

Speaking to Hollywood Life, who premiered the visual, Dylan said: “The inspiration for the ‘No Problem’ music video came to me early on. I have a lot of respect for stories and messages in music videos so I wanted to do something cool and unique.

“I imagined the girl being a ghost in the sense that she comes and goes, and leaves you wondering when she will reappear into your life and take you over. I love what the director, Roman White, did with it. Such crazy visuals paired with an awesome story.”

Watch the music video for ‘No Problem’ below.

Since its release in November, ‘No Problem’, written by Devin Dawson, Josh Hoge and Corey Crowder, has garnered almost seven million streams on Spotify alone. The track follows on from Dylan’s EP, Spotlight’s On You, which claimed the number one spot on iTunes’ Top Country Albums and reached the Top 25 on Billboard’s “Hot Country Albums” chart.

As the first country act to sign with Interscope Records, Dylan is certainly one to watch. In addition to working on new music, he is currently touring with his own headline shows. More shows are set to be announced, however, you can check out the current dates below.
