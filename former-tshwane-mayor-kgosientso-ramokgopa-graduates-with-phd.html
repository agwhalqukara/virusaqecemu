Former Tshwane mayor Kgosientso Ramokgopa graduates with PhD

The ANC GP took to social media to congratulate former mayor of Tshwane, Kgosientso Ramokgopa, for attaining his PhD.

PAY ATTENTION: Click “See First” under the “Following” tab to see We News on your News Feed!

The ruling party posted a tweet giving praise to the PEC Member and Chairperson of the ETC Subcommittee. Dr Kgosientso Ramokgopa graduated with a PhD in Public Affairs from the University of Pretoria.

Ramokgopa has a rich history with the ANC. He led the South African Student Congress (SASCO), the ANC Youth League at the University of Durban Westville and the ANC branch in Atteridgeville, and served as ANC Ward 51 Councillor (2000 to 2005).

He has received many awards and recognition including the 2008 MTN Boss of the Year, 2009 CEO

His life motto is: “Live truthfully and you shall prevail against all adversity.”
