Get to Know Skymed Star Morgan Holmstrom with These 10 Fun Facts! (Exclusive)

Morgan Holmstrom stars in the new Paramount+ series SkyMed and we caught up with her to learn some fun facts that fans might not already know.

The show revolves around life, death and drama at 20,000 feet. The series weaves together intense character journeys and high-stakes medical rescues of nurses and pilots flying air ambulances in remote Northern Canada. All nine episodes from season one are currently streaming!

Fans might also recognize Morgan from her work as Wahionhaweh on Outlander and as Sarah on Syfy’s Day of the Dead.

Check out 10 Fun Facts below:

  • I am almost always wearing baggy comfy clothes.
  • My first official job was serving at an old folks home and ended up being a manager there for a couple years.
  • I love hiking and hope to do the west coast trail very soon.
  • I taught myself how to make paper cards from scratch so I can hand make birthday cards for friends and family.
  • I took a bio-med program in my last year of high-school thinking I was going to be a doctor.

    • Click inside to read the rest of the fun facts…

  • I unfortunately really love reality TV.
  • I once begged my dad to rescue a street cat that kept coming to our house and she ended up having 10 kittens in my moms closet a few weeks later.. Sorry mom!
  • One of my favorite books is “Tuesdays with Morrie.”
  • I played violin, both classical and fiddle for over 10 years growing up. I’m a little rusty now but I’m hoping to get back into it.
  • I love animals and hope to have a hobby farm one day.
