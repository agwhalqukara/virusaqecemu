Description

Looking for some Halloween fun for your children? Head over to Sensory Seekers, Concord's inclusive sensory play gym! Children can enjoy all the fun of Sensory Seekers with extra Halloween fun on top of it including a glow-in-the-dark party. It is a no-shoe facility, but socks are required for adults. Costumes are encouraged.
