Melanie Lynskey Divorcing Jimmi Simpson

Melanie Lynskey is calling it quits with Jimmi Simpson.

The Two and a Half Men star, 35, filed for divorce from the It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia actor, 36, on September 25, TMZ reports.

Lynksey, who plays Rose on the CBS sitcom, submitted her papers to the Los Angeles County Superior Court, citing "irreconcilable differences" as the reason for their split.

The pair, who wed in 2007, have no children together.
