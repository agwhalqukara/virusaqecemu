Nigerian Man 'Makes It Rain' Biscuits on Surprised Neice, Says Uncle Is Here, Eat My Money

  • A Nigerian man who describes himself as a content creator made people laugh during a visit to his niece
  • Announcing his position as the kid's uncle, the man threw R1.25 worth of biscuits to the kid sitting on a couch
  • Many people who watched his video said they've finally found an affordable TikTok trend they to join

A young man made many people laugh as he shared a video of his niece on TikTok.

The uncle (@zubby2f_) walked into a room where the kid was sitting on a couch. He approached the girl and said:

"Uncle is here!"

Uncle buys biscuits for his niece

He opened his handbag and started "spraying" the kid with R1.25 worth of biscuits. He threw her the snacks one after the other as if they were money.

A woman behind the camera laughed at the act in the TikTok video. After touching some of the biscuits, the kid kept looking up in surprise.
