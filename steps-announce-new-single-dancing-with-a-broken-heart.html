Steps Announce New Single Dancing With A Broken Heart

Steps have announced a new single Dancing With A Broken Heart and a special deluxe edition of their charting topping album Tears On The Dancefloor as they continue to celebrate twenty years of Steps.

Dancing With A Broken Heart that will be released on September 19th. The track will feature on a very special new version of the album, Tears On The Dancefloor: Crying At The Disco Deluxe Edition, and will be released on October 27th. The special new album will also feature 4 other new tracks and the confirmed tracklisting is:

  • Dancing With A Broken Heart
  • Scared Of The Dark
  • September Sun
  • Neon Blue (Radio Mix)
  • You Make Me Whole
  • Story Of A Heart
  • Fool For You
  • Happy
  • No More Tears On The Dancefloor
  • Beautiful Battlefield
  • Firefly
  • Space Between Us
  • Glitter & Gold
  • I Will Love Again
  • Dear Santa (Bonus Track)

    • On the band’s official store, you can pre-order copies of the album, with each member face on. The albums will be signed by each band member and are limited editions.
