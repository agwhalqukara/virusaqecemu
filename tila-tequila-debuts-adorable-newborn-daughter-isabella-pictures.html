Tila Tequila Debuts Adorable Newborn Daughter Isabella: Pictures

Cutest ever! Tila Tequila posted the first pictures of her newborn daughter Isabella Monroe Nguyen on her website on Tuesday, Nov.25, and she’s seriously adorable.

In one image, the 9-day-old wears a cozy-looking hooded vest with leopard pants and a monkey hat. “So it’s been a good 9 whole days since Isabella has been born, and I know you all have been waiting to see pictures of her so I didn’t want to keep you waiting any longer!” Tequila, whose real name is real name Tila Nguyen, shared with fans.

The new mom couldn’t be more ecstatic about welcoming Isabella into the world on Nov. 16. “I am so lucky to be able to wake up seeing and kissing on this beautiful little face of hers every single day,” she wrote. “The world could end right now, and as long as I have her held close to my chest and feel her little heart beating…nothing else in this world matters!”

The former MTV reality star, 33, added that her little girl has been a breeze. “Isabella is such a good baby! She barely ever cries, and has such an easy temper, but I always knew that she would because during my pregnancy I really stayed calm, relaxed, and stress free,” she wrote.

After Tequila announced her pregnancy back in April with a bump picture on Facebook, she told Us Weekly: “I truly feel that my baby has saved my life, and I will do whatever it takes to give my little baby the whole entire world!”
