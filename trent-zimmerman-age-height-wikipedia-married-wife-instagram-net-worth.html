The younger of Roy and Brenda Zimmerman’s two children, Zimmerman was born in Sydney. Beginning in Wyvern House in 1974 and finishing his HSC in 1986, Zimmerman attended Newington College. 

After serving in the New South Wales Parliament, Zimmerman joined the federal government of John Howard as a ministerial adviser in the environment and heritage portfolio. He then served as an advisor to Joe Hockey, who was the shadow treasurer at the time. From 1992 to 1993, he served as the Young Liberal Movement of NSW’s president. Prior to the 2012 local elections, Zimmerman served two terms as a councilor on the North Sydney Council.
