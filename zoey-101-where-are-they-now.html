Zoey 101: Where Are They Now?

Nickelodeon is famous for some of the most popular TV shows, and Zoey 101 is no different. With Britney Spears’ little sister as the lead role, it was already guaranteed to be a hit. It premiered on January 9, 2005 and wrapped on May 2, 2008.

It was a show about Zoey Brooks (Jamie Lynn Spears) who attended a fictional boarding school named Pacific Coast Academy (PCA). The series is actually one of the most costly shows Nickelodeon ever did seeing as it was filmed 100% on location in schools in Southern California.

So, where are the cast members now?

Alexa Nikolas (Nicole)

Alexa was cast on the original cast of Zoey 101, but after many arguments with Jamie Lynn Spears, she was fired and written out of the show before the third season.

Since Zoey 101, Alexa did many shows like ER, Suite Life of Zack and Cody, and even played Haley from the hit TV show The Walking Dead.

Christopher Massey (Michael)

Massey (who is the older brother of Kyle Massey) stayed for all seasons and movies of Zoey 101. Since Zoey 101, he has continued to rap and appear on some TV shows like Switched at Birth.

Paul Butcher (Dustin)

Paul played Dustin Brooks, Zoey Brooks’ brother, in Zoey 101 for all seasons. Since the show ended, he did many voice acting roles in movies like Avatar: The Last Airbender, Over The Hedge and Meet The Robinsons. Most recently he played Tyler Cunningham in Comedy Bang Bang.

Matthew Underwood (Logan)

Matthew played Logan in all seasons of Zoey 101 and was a main role on the show. He was the show’s heartthrob. He retired from acting in 2009 and has become a performing arts teacher at “Academy of Entertainment” in Florida.

Sean Flynn (Chase)

Sean played the boy madly in love with Zoey and was always just her best friend. Since Zoey 101 he had a role on Devious Maids as Jason and was part of the Zoey 101 reboot mini episode called “What Did Zoey Say”.

Erin Sanders (Quinn)

Quinn had a main part in Zoey 101 as one of Zoey’s best friends and was that lovely little nerdy girl we all adored. Since the show ended Erin has a regular role on The Young and The Restless as Eden. She also appeared on mainly one-time roles for shows like CSI: Miami and Melissa and Joey.

Victoria Justice (Lola)

Victoria had a main role on the show as Lola for 52 episodes. She is most known for this role and was then given a show of her own that went on to be extremely popular called Victorious, also aired on Nickelodeon. Since Zoey 101 and Victorious ended, she’s been on shows like Eye Candy, Undateable and on Lip Sync Battle.

Jamie Lynn Spears (Zoey)

Jamie Lynn Spears was the lead character in Zoey 101 and was the reason we all flocked to watch the show. Being the little sister of famous popstar Britney Spears is something to be proud of. Jamie Lynn went on to have a lucrative singing career and has since become a mom. She had a controversial teen pregnancy, but didn’t let that stop her from becoming a well known name in the industry while still being an A+ parent to her daughter.
